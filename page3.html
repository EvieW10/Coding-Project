Camp Rock

2008 - Family/Musical - 1h 37m

Quick Facts:

When it debuted in 2008, Camp Rock had 8.9 million viewers and became the most-watched non-sequel movie on Disney Channel.

Camp Rock's soundtrack was and still is one of the most notable Disney successes, as the movie's single "This is Me" peaked at #9 on the Billboard 100.


Summary:

Mitchie Torres finally gets the opportunity to attend her dream summer camp, but she has to work with her mom in the kitchen in order to afford the prestigous Camp Rock. She is overheard singing by teen pop star Shane Gray, who sets out to find the owner of such an enchanting voice.
