High School Musical

2006 - Family/Musical - 1h 38m

Home Extras Collection

Quick Facts:

High School Musical premiered on January 20, 2006 with around 7.7 million viewers. The second installment drew almost 20 million viewers.

The movie is set in Albuquerque, New Mexico, however, it was flimed in Salt Lake City, Utah.


Summary:

High School Musical follows Troy Bolton, East High's star basketball player, and Gabriella Montez as they enter the world of musical theater, and learn the importance of authenticity.
